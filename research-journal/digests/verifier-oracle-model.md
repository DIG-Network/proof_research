## Confirmed dead ends (straw models / relaxations)

- Verifier ignoring **`Link`** / on-chain **C** / message **m** / distinct indices / **K** vs leaf-sum (**002**, **013вҖ“018**, **020** in legacy log).
- Pure **linear FвӮӮ pooling** without extra structure cannot **alone** separate threshold patterns in the exhibited pair (**021**).
- **Parity of total signer count** as the **only** datum cannot decide **k вүҘ t** (**023**).
- **Tropical** **min-only** **summary** **min_{iвҲҲS}c_i** **(public** **strict** **c_i)** **aliases** **across** **t** **if** **argmin** **index** **lies** **in** **both** **witness** **sets** вҖ” **030**.
- **Adjacency (О»_max, О»_min)** alone on **G[S]** fails on **star** when **S** is **leaf-only** (empty induced graph) вҖ” **025**; same pattern on **dense** **K_{3,7}** when **S** lies in the **7**-vertex part вҖ” **027**.
